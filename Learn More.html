ABOUT US

Despite being one of the world's top social media users, the Philippines faces alarmingly low levels of ICT literacy. As of 2019, only 6% of people aged 15 and above have basic internet skills, with even fewer possessing standard or advanced digital skills. This has led to a surge in students choosing ICT as their major in senior high school (SHS). However, the pressure to make career choices that will impact their future success can be overwhelming for these students.

To address this challenge, the Achieve IT web portal has been developed to guide STI senior high school ICT students in Sta. Mesa. Achieve IT offers individualized recommendations, detailed analyses, and valuable resources to help students navigate the technology industry. Utilizing the latest advances in educational technology, it equips students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to embark on their careers with clarity and conviction, ensuring they are well-prepared for the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
